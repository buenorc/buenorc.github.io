PhD student in the Graduate Program of Environmental Engineering (PPGEA) at the Federal University of Paraná (UFPR) and at University of Koblenz-Landau (Germany) in the environmental physics group (Institute for environmental sciences). Member of the Research Group of Environmental Monitoring, Modeling and Management of Reservoirs, Estuaries and Catchments (REMARE). B.Eng and M.Eng in Environmental Engineering from Federal University of Paraná with a long term in Institute of Technology Tralee (Ireland). Research is focused on physical limnology and environmental fluid mechanics, with major interest in the hydrodynamic of stratified flows.
