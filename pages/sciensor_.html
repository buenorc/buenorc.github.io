Sciensor | Meio Ambiente & Inovação

Sciensor is a company founded in 2022 by me and some friends in order to provide innovative technology to environmental consulting services. The idea was concepted by me, my lifelong friend (Dr. Andre Luiz Diniz dos Santos), and my Ph.D. advisor (Dr. Eng. Tobias Bleninger) at the Federal University of Paraná in 2019. In the mean time, we structured the company and our goals, inviting some colegues to be part of our team. Sciensor was built to be not a ordinary company that provides environmental consulting services, but to provide custom technological solution for our clients. The companny is structured in four different business areas, and a non-profit foundation: Smart projects, Innova projects, Consul projects, Sciensor Academy, and Sciens Foundation.

The idea of Innova projects is to create a bridge between universities, companies, and non-profit foundations. Environmental resources management and protection are at the core of sustainable development, and require a constant monitoring and assessing, being a key element to minimize the negative impacts on ecosystems. Innova projects are designed to do reseach on sensors and software development to guaratee the well-beign of our enviroment creating innovative tools to help the environmental monitoring and mitigation.

Sciens Foundation has a different goal. The foundation, powered by Sciensor, is a non-profit foundation that does not have the intention to explore finantially services and products developed by us. At Sciens, all projects are designed to improve education system, which for us is a main pillar to build a society based on knowledge, that may contribute strongly to the environmental protection, political decisions, economic and technological development, and to eliminate all forms of discrimination and intolerance (gender inequality, racial discrimination, sexual orientation discrimination, among others).
