Welcome to Limnotronic Laboratory, a dynamic and forward-thinking research group at the forefront of cutting-edge science.
Here, we unite the realms of physical limnology and sensor development to address pressing environmental issues

At Limnotronic, our collective drive stems from a shared passion for comprehending and conserving diverse ecosystems. Through the synergy of environmental monitoring, the Internet of Things, and software development, we convert raw data into practical intelligence, aiding in the monitoring and mitigation of environmental impacts. A pivotal aspect of our research revolves around delving into the realm of physical limnology. By investigating the dynamics of stratified flows, we unearth valuable insights into the fundamental workings of lakes as crucial ecological systems. Our discoveries provide a comprehensive understanding of the biogeochemical fluxes within these ecosystems, exerting a profound influence on biogeochemical cycles and water quality. The seamless integration of sensor development and physical limnology serves as the propelling force behind our quest for sustainable solutions.
